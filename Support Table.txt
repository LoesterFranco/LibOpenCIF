SUPPORT TABLE
=============

Here  the  reader  can  find  a table of supported systems and versions on wich
LibOpenCIF was tested and certified. Being certified means that the library can
be  compiled, installed (only on non-Windows systems) and integrated into final
programs.  All  the  systems  tested  were  updated  to  their  latest versions
according to the date specified in each entry.
